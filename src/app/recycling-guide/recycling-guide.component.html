Recycling Guide

Learn how to sort and recycle waste efficiently to contribute to a cleaner environment.

How to Sort Your Waste

  • Plastics: Place clean plastic bottles, containers, and bags in the recycling bin.
  • Paper: Recycle newspapers, magazines, cardboard, and office paper.
  • Glass: Rinse and recycle glass bottles and jars.
  • Metal: Recycle aluminum cans, tin cans, and other metal items.

Items That Cannot Be Recycled

  • Plastic bags with food residue
  • Styrofoam
  • Dirty pizza boxes
  • Light bulbs and batteries

Recycling Tips

Always rinse recyclables to remove food and liquids. Flatten cardboard boxes to save space.
